Leonid Parfyonov

Russian journalist, news presenter, TV producer and author of many documentary TV shows. From December 3, 2004 until December 20, 2007 he was an Editor-in-Chief of Russky Newsweek, Russian edition of Newsweek. Parfyonov is currently a member of Presidential Council for Civil Society and Human Rights.

Nowdays Leonid runs a popular YouTube Channel for wine and news lovers.
